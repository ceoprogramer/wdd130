Mexican River Rafting

Raft the South Fork or the Middle Fork of the Mexico River for a classic Amacuzac whitewater experience. Located between Mexico City and Cuernavaca, both rivers are easily accessible, offering a variety of 1-Day and overnight trips to all types of adventurers.
